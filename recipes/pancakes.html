High Protein Egg White Pancakes

Quick egg white pancakes by Matteo posted on mattsfitchef.com. Makes 1 serving. Jump to Macros

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Egg Whites (or 1/2 Cup Liquid Egg Whites)
  • 1/2 Cup Flour (of choice)
  • 1/3 Cup Greek Yogurt (2% or full fat recommended)
  • 1 Tbsp Maple Syrup

Instructions

  1. Place egg whites in a large bowl and beat with handmixer until stiff peaks form
  2. In a seperate bowl, combine flour, greek yogurt, and syrup until smooth
  3. Using a spatula, slowly fold egg whites into mixture, careful to not overmix
  4. Heat pan and lightly grease
  5. Once hot, cook pancakes until bubble form and underside is golden
  6. Flip and cook otherside until both sides are golden brown, careful not to overcook
  7. Plate and top with syrup and/or fruit of choice

Additional Information

Handmixer or whisk needed to beat egg whites

Approximate Macros Per Serving

  • Calories: 370
  • Fat: 5g
  • Carbs: 52g
  • Protien: 28g
