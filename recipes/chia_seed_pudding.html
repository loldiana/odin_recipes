Chia Seed Pudding

Simple Chia Seed pudding recipe. Jump to Macros.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Chia Seeds
  • 1/2 Cup Milk of Choice
  • 2 tsp Sweetener of Choice
  • Pinch of Salt (optional)
  • Chopped Fruit of Choice (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl or mason jar, combine chia seeds, milk, sweetener, and Salt
  2. Stir or add lid and shake well
  3. Let sit for abour 10 minutes
  4. Stir from bottom up
  5. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours - overnight, until pudding thickens
  6. Stir and top with (optional) chopped fruit and/or sweetener of choice
  7. Serve

Additional Information

Add milk if pudding is too thick, add seeds if too thin.

Refrigerate for up to 5 days

Approximate Macros Per Serving

  • Fat: 9g
